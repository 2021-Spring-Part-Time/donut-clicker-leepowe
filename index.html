Hello, Welcome to Donut Clicker.

We are the dreamers of dreams. We are the Champions. We welcome you to the world of donuts. For your donut loving entertainment. LAAAAAAAAADDDDIES and gentlemen, boys and girls, children of all ages. Fred 'the Baker' Baking Company proudly brings to you Donut Clicker: The Home Game!

Each click without a multiplier will make one donut.

Donuts Made:

Your first auto clicker will cost 100 donuts and make 1 donut every second.
Each subsequent auto clicker will cost an extra 10 percent.

Auto Clickers Purchased:

Your first multiplier will cost 10 donuts and each multiplier increase the amount of donuts made from each click by 20 percent.
The cost of subsequent multipliers will increase by 10 percent.
